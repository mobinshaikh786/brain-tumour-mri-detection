🧠 About the Project

NeuroDetect AI is a web-based application developed as a major project for BCA program at TMU Moradabad. It uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze brain MRI images and classify them into Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary Tumor, and No Tumor.

AI-powered Brain Tumor Detection System for early diagnosis and assistance.
